Title: Banana Protein Shake

Ingredients:
- 1 banana 
- 1 pack of flavoured yogurt (optional) 
- 1 scoop of protein powder (optional) 
- 1 handful of blueberries 
- 1/2 to 3/4 cup of Greek yogurt 
- 1 teaspoon of peanut butter 
- 3% milk (add to preference) 
- 1/4 cup of oats

Instructions: Mix together in a blender!
