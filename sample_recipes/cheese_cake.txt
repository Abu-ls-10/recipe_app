Title: Cheese Cake

Ingredients:
  Cake:
  - 500g cream cheese (room temp)
  - 1 cup sugar
  - 1 tbsp cornstarch
  - Salt
  - 1/2 cup sour cream
  - 2 tsp fresh lemon juice
  - Vanilla extract
  - 3 large eggs

  Base:
  - Graham crackers
  - Butter

  Topping:
  - Whipped cream
  - (Strawberries + Strawberry jam) OR (Pineapples + pineapple jam)

Instructions:
1. Bake base at 350 for 8-10 minutes
2. Pour cake batter in base
3. Bake cake at 350 for 50-60 mins
4. Add pan of water in oven to keep cake moist
