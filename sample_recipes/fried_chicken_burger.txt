Title: Fried Chicken Burger

Ingredients:
  Dry powder:
  - 2 cups flour
  - 2 tbsp paprika 
  - 1 tbsp chilli powder
  - Black pepper
  - Salt

  Wet coating:
  - 4 Eggs

  Sauce: 
  - 1/4 cup mayo
  - 2 tbsp sriracha sauce 
  - 1/4 cup honey
  - Cilantro/parsley/chives

Instructions:
1. Marinate chicken breasts in pickle or lemon juice
2. Coat chicken in dry powder
3. Dip chicken in eggs
4. Coat chicken with corn flakes
5. Serve in burger with pickles, bell peppers, cheese, extra mayo
